About Me

Hello! I'm Akbar Makhmanazarov, a 27-year-old IT Specialist based in Szeged, Hungary, originally from Uzbekistan.

Currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of Szeged (SZTE), I work simultaneously as an IT Specialist at SGS, Web Developer at Agora Election Observation Organization, and Cyber Security Researcher at British Airways.

My journey in tech is driven by a passion for cybersecurity, penetration testing, and network security. From Cisco networking and TCP/IP protocols to ethical hacking and vulnerability assessment, I'm dedicated to securing digital infrastructure.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me participating in intellectual events with Team Gravity, or gaming - because yes, I play games more than I code, but I love both!
